#### <a name="how-do-i-upgrade"></a><span data-ttu-id="167bb-195">Wie aktualisiere ich?</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="167bb-195">How do I upgrade?</span></span>

After you begin an in-place upgrade, you cannot pause the upgrade or roll back to the previous version.
