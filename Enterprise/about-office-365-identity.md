<span data-ttu-id="c2f70-139">Erfahren Sie mehr über die [Auswahl der Passthrough-Authentifizierung](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/security/azure-ad-choose-authn) und [des nahtlosen einmaligen Anmeldens](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/active-directory/connect/active-directory-aadconnect-sso).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2f70-139">Learn more about [choosing pass-through authentication](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/security/azure-ad-choose-authn) and [seamless single sign-on](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/active-directory/connect/active-directory-aadconnect-sso).</span></span>
